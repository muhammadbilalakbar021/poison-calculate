Acetaminophen Poisoning Calculator

This program is built for determining and calculating toxic ingested doses and toxic acetaminophen concentrations in acute poisoning and supratherapeutic ingestion of acetaminophen. We used Rumack's nomogram and other guidelines in countries like the US, UK, Australia, etc., and considered many scenarios, including non-toxic, toxic, massive ingestion, etc. In addition, this program will provide a suggestion for each condition.
